About

Avenhade is the result of 10 years of musical exploration, trying things, messing up, starting over. I needed to create something big emotionally, something that didn’t care about fitting into one genre. The sound blends a bit of everything : Alt and prog rock, orchestral arrangements, ambient textures, electronic layers… It’s modern, but it’s not chasing trends. Just trying to say something real. Distorted guitars crash into string sections, with synths, pianos, and deep percussion building the atmosphere. Each track is more like a journey than a song, just something that evolves. This identity took years to figure out. I had to break things apart, rebuild them, and let a lot of personal moments shape the music. My debut album isn’t just a collection of tracks, it’s the end of a long search, and the start of something new. That’s Avenhade’s vision: powerful, melodic, evocative, and unafraid to dive deep.
